Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Expense Manager respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and safeguard your data.

2. Information We Collect

Expense Manager does not collect, store, or transmit personal financial data to external servers.

All financial information such as income, expenses, and account balances is stored locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored on your device using local storage mechanisms. We do not access or monitor your financial records.

4. Permissions

The app may request certain permissions to function properly, such as:

  • Internet access (for opening website links)
  • Notification access (for reminders)

These permissions are used only for intended functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Expense Manager does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
